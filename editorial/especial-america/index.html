From Brazil to the United States

“ESPECIAL AMÉRICA” was an incredible combination between:
ARTEFACTO - FLORENCE - ORNARE - SACCARO

These outstanding brands “Made in Brazil”, have an special common value; The absolute success of having stores in the United States. It was a great and pleasurable challenge bring together all the best of theses successful brands and BUZZ loud all over the US about. I was able to speak daily with the brand’s art directors, designers, editors. This exchange of information was enriching.
The results were incredibly valuable because we were able to bring new looks to the brands in American territory. Another super important point about these campaigns was the fact that it was published in decoration magazines all over the US.
