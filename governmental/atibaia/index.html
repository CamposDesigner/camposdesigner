ATIBAIA

A new administration had taken over the city of Atibaia, and to rescue the pride of the people of the city, the Campaign “Viva Atibaia” was produced with all the cultural elements and historical qualities of the city.
Named as the most popular campaign by the population of Atibaia (Aired on TVs, Radios, Newspapers, Magazines, Web). “Viva Atibaia” has become a milestone in the recent history of this beautiful city.
