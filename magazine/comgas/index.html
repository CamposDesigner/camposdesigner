COMGÁS

The numbers are powerful. Do the math: the largest distributor of piped natural gas in Brazil, Comgás has been in the market for more than 140 years, bringing practicality, safety and comfort to more than one million and one hundred thousand customers. There are more than seven thousand kilometers of scattered networks in 68 municipalities in the Metropolitan Region of São Paulo, Campinas, Baixada Santista and Vale do Paraiba.
An equation that could only result in a stratospheric recipe for success, credibility and happiness, given the weaknesses that you will feel seeing this magazine, under the art direction of designer Luciano Campos.
